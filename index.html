SalamViz – A Vision by Faisal Salam

I'm Faisal Salam, the founder and CEO of SalamViz. My journey in the CG industry began back in 2015. Over the years, I’ve worn many hats from animator to technical artist, VFX compositor, and architectural visualizer. Each role taught me something unique, and all those experiences laid the foundation for what would become SalamViz. I started this studio with just three passionate artists and a clear focus: architectural visualization. What began as a small team has now grown into a close-knit family of eight incredibly talented 3D artists, each bringing their own flair and expertise to the table. At SalamViz, we believe in a fully collaborative approach. Every project is a blend of creativity, technical precision, and bold imagination. Our strength lies in the diverse skills of our team we learn from each other, challenge each other, and grow together.
